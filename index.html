About Us:

After seeing the amount of food waste in the cafes of our university, we decided it wouldn't be fair not to take action. We are a small student-run organization that collects unsold items from outlets across King's College London and gives them away to less fortunate individuals - via outreaches and soup kitchens. Issues of food waste and homelessness are both extremely complex and multi-faceted but we are happy even if one sandwich is rescued - it can truly make someone's day.
